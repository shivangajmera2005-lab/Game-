Laws Of The Empire

Gameplay Rules

Know the laws. Master the game. Break the wrong rule and your empire falls.

01
🏰 Empire Progression
Advance your pieces through the four levels of the triangular empire board. Each level demands more resources and strategic cunning than the last.
02
🃏 Card Play
Deploy cards strategically from your hand. Attack rivals, defend segments, sabotage enemies, or trigger powerful events. Each card type serves a distinct strategic purpose.
03
🚩 Flag System
Every player begins with 5 flags. Expand up to a maximum of 12. Additional flags cost 2 tokens each. Flags represent your empire's territorial reach.
04
💰 Token Management
Tokens are your lifeblood. Spend them to acquire higher-rarity cards. Never let your reserves fall below the minimum of 3 or face elimination from the empire wars.
05
⚔ Combat System
Attack and Defence cards clash in power-based combat. Card power values range from 2 to 7. Superior power wins. Choose your battles carefully — resources are finite.
06
💀 Bankruptcy & Elimination
Fall below 3 tokens and your empire collapses. No player may offer or receive loans. Bankruptcy means permanent elimination.
⚠ MINIMUM 3 TOKENS AT ALL TIMES

Mastery

Strategy & Risk

The difference between an emperor and a footnote is strategy. Master these principles to reign supreme.

🏹
Aggressive Expansion
Strike early and strike hard. Use attack cards to displace rivals from key segments before they fortify. Speed is your greatest weapon in the early empire.
🛡
Defensive Fortress
Build an impenetrable wall of defence cards. Let others drain resources attacking each other while you conserve tokens for the critical final push.
💰
Token Conservation
Never spend what you don't need to. Token efficiency is the hallmark of a true strategist. Keep rivals near bankruptcy while staying safely above 3.
🕵
Shadow Sabotage
Deploy sabotage cards against the leader, not the weakest. Disrupt flag systems and use chaos as your greatest ally on the climb to the throne.
🚩
Flag Dominance
Control the board through flag superiority. Expanded territory means more presence, more options, and more paths to the Golden Throne.
⚡
Event Exploitation
Read event cards for opportunity, not just threat. Every catastrophe for your rivals is a window for your ascent. Turn chaos into conquest.
Questions

Frequently Asked

How many players can participate in Empire Climb?
+
Empire Climb supports 3 to 6 players. With 3 players, competition is intense and direct. With 6, the board becomes a chaotic battlefield of shifting alliances and devastating betrayals.
What happens if I run out of tokens?
+
If your token count drops below 3 at any point, your empire is declared bankrupt and you are permanently eliminated. No loans can be given or received. Manage your treasury with absolute care.
How does the card power system work?
+
Attack and Defence cards have power values ranging from 2 to 7, displayed in the top-right corner. When cards clash in combat, the higher power value wins. Power Cards and Sabotage Cards have unique special abilities instead of numeric power.
What are Dot Cards used for?
+
20 Dot Cards exist across four colors — Golden, Red, Yellow, and Blue. Golden Dot Cards are legendary and extremely rare. Their specific game function will be detailed in the full rulebook.
Can I buy more flags during the game?
+
Yes. Every player starts with 5 flags and can purchase additional flags at a cost of 2 tokens each. The absolute maximum is 12 flags per player. Each flag purchase depletes your treasury — plan carefully.
What makes Golden Cards so special?
+
Golden Cards represent the highest tier in the token economy, costing 8 tokens to acquire. They are the most powerful cards in the game and can dramatically shift the balance of power. Use them at the decisive moment.
